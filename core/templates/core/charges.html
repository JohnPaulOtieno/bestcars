How KRA Calculates Import Duty

Kenya Revenue Authority uses a set of standard inputs to determine the taxable value of your imported vehicle. Understanding these helps you plan your budget accurately.

KRA
Your Tax

CRSP Value

KRA's Current Retail Selling Price — a benchmark value they set for each vehicle model, independent of what you actually paid at auction.

Depreciation

A depreciation rate is applied based on the age of the vehicle, reducing the taxable base. Newer vehicles have higher duty; older ones benefit from depreciation allowances.

Engine & Fuel

Engine size and fuel type determine which excise duty band applies. Larger engines and certain fuel types attract higher excise rates.

Mileage

Any vehicle with over 1,000 km on the odometer is classified as a used vehicle for valuation purposes — which affects how the duty is computed.
